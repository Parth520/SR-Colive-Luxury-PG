About SR Colive
Luxury PG

SR Colive Luxury PG offers a premium co-living experience designed for comfort, convenience, and security. Our PG provides high-quality accommodation with modern facilities, hygienic homemade meals, and a safe environment with 24/7 support.

Located in a convenient area, it is ideal for professionals and students looking for a peaceful and well-managed living space. Every detail has been thoughtfully curated to make you feel at home — from your private furnished room to our communal dining experience.
